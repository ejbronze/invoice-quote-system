Getting Started

How do I create a new quote or invoice?

From the home screen, click New Quote or New Invoice in the top toolbar. The editor opens in a step-by-step flow — fill in document details, client info, line items, keywords, then review before exporting.

How do I edit an existing document?

Click the pencil icon on any document card, or click Edit inside the PDF preview window. The editor reopens with all fields intact and ready to update.

Edit button Edit
How do I set up my company profile?

Open Tools from the top toolbar, then click Open Company Profile. Your name, address, logo, letterhead, and payment info will appear on every document you generate.

Documents & Exporting

What is the difference between a Quote and an Invoice?

A Quote is a price estimate sent before work begins. An Invoice is a formal payment request issued after work is completed or goods are delivered. You can switch between the two in Step 1 of the editor.

How do I export a document to PDF?

Click the eye icon on any document card to open the PDF preview window, then use your browser's print dialog to save as PDF. You can also reach the preview from Step 6 – Final Preview inside the editor.

Open PDF preview Preview PDF
Can I save line items to reuse later?

Yes. In Step 3, use the cart icon next to Add Item to open your saved items. Any item saved from a previous document appears there and can be added with one click. The Catalog page aggregates all captured items across documents.

How do I add or update payment terms?

Scroll to the Document Notes & Terms panel at the bottom of Step 3. The Terms of Payment field is pre-filled with a NET 15 default — update it to match your agreement.

How do I back up or restore my data?

Open Tools and use the JSON Backup option to export a full snapshot of your documents and clients. Use JSON Restore to import a previously saved backup. CSV import is also available for bulk document creation.

Document Cards

What do the action icons on document cards do?

Each card shows two icon buttons on the right. The eye icon opens the PDF preview in a new window. The pencil icon opens the document in the editor. A three-dot menu gives access to additional actions like payment status, type conversion, and delete.

Card action buttons Preview PDF Edit
How do I use the PDF preview window?

The preview window shows the print-ready document. Use Print to open your browser's print dialog and save as PDF. Use Edit to jump back to the editor with the document loaded. Close Preview dismisses the window.

How do I filter documents by type?

Use the All / Quotes / Invoices / Statements tabs directly below the search bar. Clicking a tab shows only documents of that type in the same view — no page reload. The Statements tab shows previously generated Statement of Account exports.

How do I mark an invoice as paid?

On the home screen, open the invoice card's three-dot menu and select the appropriate payment status — Unpaid, Pending, or Paid. Paid invoices are visually flagged and excluded from outstanding balance calculations in Invoice Reports.

Clients & Statements

How do I save a client for reuse?

In Step 2 of the editor, enter the client name and address, then click Save Client. Saved clients appear in the dropdown on future documents so you don't need to retype their details. Switching clients also restores their saved consignee fields.

What is a Statement of Account?

A Statement of Account is a branded PDF summary of selected invoices for a specific client — useful for reconciliation and payment follow-up. Access it from the Invoice Reports section. Generated statements are saved and appear under the Statements tab on the home screen.

What do the action icons on statement rows do?

Each saved statement has three action buttons. The eye icon reopens the PDF export. The pencil icon opens the statement editor so you can adjust it. The trash icon permanently deletes the saved statement.

Statement action buttons Open Edit Delete

Tips & Shortcuts

How do I switch the display language?

Use the language selector in the top toolbar (EN / ES / FR). The interface and generated document labels will update to match your selected language immediately.

Can I add my company logo and signature to documents?

Yes. Open Company Profile from the Tools menu. You can upload a letterhead image, signature, stamp, and footer wave — all of which appear in exported PDFs automatically.

How do I search for a document?

Use the search bar at the top of the home screen. You can search by client name, reference number, date, or any keyword added in Step 4 of the editor. The search works across all document types.

How do I use the Catalog?

Click Catalog in the top navigation. It shows every line item captured from quotes, invoices, and the cart. You can also add manual catalog entries. Items in the catalog can be inserted into new documents quickly from the cart.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Click Submit / Report Issues in the footer. Fill in a summary, add any relevant screenshots, and submit. Your report is logged and visible to the admin in the Service Reports panel under Tools.
