About SantoSync

SantoSync

SantoSync is a coined name designed to feel steady, premium, and synchronized for modern trade operations.

SantoSync is a premium document workspace for teams that need quotes, invoices, and business identity details to stay aligned from first draft to final export.

Designed and developed by Edwin Jaquez through Palmchat Innovations Lab under Palmchat Innovations LLC NYC, with a focus on premium operational tools for modern trade and logistics teams.
